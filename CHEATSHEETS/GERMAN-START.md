## 1. Pronunciation Master Section  

### Quick Rules + Fixes (Memorize First)

- **Vowels**  
  A → ah (Vater) | E long ay (See) / short eh (Bett)  
  I long ee (Lied) / short ih (ist)  
  O → oh (Boot) | U → oo rounded (Buch)  
  Ä → eh (Mädchen) | Ö → shurn (schön) | Ü → myy-deh (müde)  

- **Diphthongs**  
  ei/ai → eye (ein) | eu/äu → oy (heute) | au → ow (Haus) | ie → long ee (nie)  

- **Consonants**  
  R → uvular/gargled | Final consonants devoiced (Tag = tak, und = unt)  
  ch: ich = ç (hiss) / ach = χ (loch) | sp/st → shp/sht (Stein, Straße)  
  v = f (Vogel) | w = v (Wasser)
